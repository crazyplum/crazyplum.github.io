Hi, I'm Mei-Hua Pan

I'm a master student in CSC Lab, NTHU.
My research interests lie in the area of computer supported cooperative
work and computer mediated communication. My current projects include
supporting non-native speaker in multilingual collaboration and asynchronous
collaboration for second language reading.
Besides research, I'm also interested in Chinese modern literature and sociolinguistics.
